# Western vs Chinese Luxury Branding Logic

## Core Memory Points

- Western logic: heritage/provenance, individualism (self-expression), exclusivity through scarcity, craftsmanship narrative, understated 'quiet luxury'
- Chinese logic: Mianzi (face) — luxury as social capital and hierarchy signaling; Guanxi — luxury as gift/relationship currency; Guochao — cultural authenticity demanded since 2018
- Chinese digital-first: WeChat, Douyin, Xiaohongshu, Tmall Luxury Pavilion primary touchpoints; no meaningful offline-only consumer under 45
