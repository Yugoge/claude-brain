# Mianzi (面子) as Brand Equity Driver in China

## Core Memory Points

- Western brand equity models (e.g., Keller CBBE) incomplete for China — two unique factors: brand popularity and brand mianzi
- Brand mianzi = 2nd most important equity determinant in China (after brand loyalty)
- Mianzi mediates country-of-origin → brand equity: French heritage strengthens mianzi which drives equity
